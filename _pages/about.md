I am Huiqi Zou (邹惠琪，Anna), a senior <a href='https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk'>Computer Science </a> student at <a href="https://www.cityu.edu.hk"> City University of Hong Kong</a> (Artificial Intelligence Stream). <br>
I am advised by <a href='https://sites.google.com/site/aisquaredlab/'>Prof. Linqi Song</a>.<br>

My research interest lies in natural language processing (NLP), human-computer interaction (HCI), and multimodal learning. Previously, I developed an attention-based <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/publication/2022-ATTSUM'> bug report summarization model</a> with an encoder-decoder structure and a <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/publication/2022-ICSR_ToM'>dialogue system</a> for generating natural feedback in robot-mediated cooperative communication tasks. In my ongoing research, I am working on a project to induce human emotion based on facial expressions. My ultimate goal is to develop a system that can understand human behaviors via multimodal communication to engage people and generate reasonable responses.

For more information, please see my  <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/files/CV.pdf'>CV</a>.
