I am Huiqi Zou (邹惠琪, Anna), a Computer Science Master student at <a href="https://www.cs.jhu.edu">Johns Hopkins University</a>. I obtained my bachelor degree in Computer Science from <a href="https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk">City University of Hong Kong</a> (Artificial Intelligence Stream) and was advised by <a href='https://sites.google.com/site/aisquaredlab/'>Prof. Linqi Song</a>.<br>

My research interest lies in human-computer interaction (HCI) and natural language processing (NLP). Previously, I developed an attention-based <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/publication/2022-ATTSUM'> bug report summarization model</a> with an encoder-decoder structure and a <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/publication/2022-ICSR_ToM'>dialogue system</a> for generating natural feedback in robot-mediated cooperative communication tasks. I also investigated the influence of the <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/publication/2023-Resampling'>sampling method</a> on deep learning based anomaly detection. In my ongoing research, I am developing an agent that can evaluate the effectiveness of different personality prompts in an open domain.
In my spare time, I assisted with organizing thematic cross-culture <a href='https://hk.korean-culture.org/en/1071/board/682/read/109688'>calligraphy exhibitions</a> in Hong Kong and published a <a href='https://scholars.cityu.edu.hk/en/clippings/----114-p2526(aaf4c64c-411d-4c32-baee-3948871c6cef).html'>prose</a> in a literary magazine.

For more information, please see my <a href='https://annazou1103.github.io/files/CV.pdf'>CV</a>.

Feel free to contact me!
